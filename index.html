My story

A journalist turned full-stack developer seeking opportunities to engineer useful and intuitive online experiences.


Check out apps I've built so far on my portfolio page. I'm proficient in JavaScript and JS frameworks including React and jQuery. I've used CSS, Bootstrap, Bulma and Materialize in my front-end work. On the back end, I'm proficient with Node.js, mySQL, MongoDB, Express, Firebase and Handlebars. I learn fast and work well on teams.


As a journalist, I was dedicated to making information engaging and easy to understand. I started out copy editing and designing newspaper pages, where I developed design chops and a sharp eye for errors. I later spent more than four years engaging readers on social media and working to increase web traffic at Maine's largest newspaper. Using engagement data, I created an engagement strategy that increased our Facebook following from 21,000 to 120,000 in my time there.


As a web developer, I want to create fast, smart, intuitive products that meet users where they are.
